# Settings to (Better) Secure Your Signal

## Guidance:
- Don't send anything you don't want read back to you in court.
- Have in-person conversations as often as reasonable.
- If you're in a group chat with a bunch of people you don't know, assume it is not secure.

## Settings

Beyond using your judgement, here are some helpful settings to best maintain the integrity of your Signal messages.

### Signal Settings
- Accounts > registration lock: on
- Chats > generate link previews: off
- Chats > share contacts w/OS: off
- Chats: Use Phone Contact Photos: off
- Notifications > show > no name or content (super important!)

### Signal Settings > Privacy
- Phone Number: Set both to Nobody
- Read Receipts: off
- Typing Indicators: off
- Disappearing messages: choose a short time period
- Hide Screen in App Switcher: on
- Screen Lock: on
- Screen Lock Timeout: Instant (disable biometrics)
- Show Calls In Recents: off
- Advanced: Always Relay Calls: on
- Incognito keyboard: on (Android only)

### Phone Settings:

- Notifications > Disable notifications for Signal
- Set unlock to require a 6 digit passcode (minimum) - require passcode even if you use face/touch
